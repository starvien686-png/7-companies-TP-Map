Key Adjustments & Rationale 主要調整與理由

G1 - FCFC

Action: Move significantly UP.

FCFC is a massive multinational heavily reliant on global exports. It should sit high in the "International" quadrant, closely tracking alongside FPC.

建議：在 Y 軸上大幅向上移動。

台化（FCFC）是一家高度依賴全球出口的大型跨國企業。它應該與台塑（FPC）一起被放置在「國際範圍」的高位。

G3 - Kura Sushi

Action: Move UP & slightly RIGHT.

As a Japanese multinational, its scope is highly global. Their heavy reliance on operational tech (sushi robots, automated belts, RFID tracking) makes them noticeably more advanced than traditional food service.

建議：向上移動並微向右移。

作為跨國企業，其國際化程度極高。他們大量使用自動化技術（壽司機器人、自動化輸送帶、RFID追蹤），使其比傳統餐飲更偏向「先進技術」。

G6 - TOYOTA

Action: Shift slightly RIGHT.

Toyota bridges the gap between traditional heavy industry and advanced tech through their leadership in hybrid technology, solid-state battery R&D, and autonomous driving AI.

建議：稍微向右移向先進技術。

豐田透過在混合動力技術、固態電池研發和自動駕駛 AI 的領先地位，跨越了傳統重工業與先進科技之間的界線。
